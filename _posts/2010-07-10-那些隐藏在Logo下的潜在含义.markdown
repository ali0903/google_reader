## 那些隐藏在Logo下的潜在含义
### by 
### at 2010-07-10 18:17:18
### original <http://news.cnblogs.com/n/67998/>

<p>一个简单的Logo设计往往传递着很多潜在信息，作为一个设计师，我们需要能够察觉到这些隐藏的含义。本文将解释那些伟大的Logo背后的<a href="http://cheweb.org/521.html">潜在含义</a>，希望这对你的将来的设计有帮助。</p>
<p>我在这里说明一下，老外对名称的首字母和缩写很在意，因此很多Logo都会隐藏的传递这两个信息。</p>
<h3>Amazon.com</h3>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image.png"><img style="border-width:0px;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="74"></a></p>
<p>A和Z下面的箭头暗示着Amazon销售一切可销售的商品，这个箭头同时是个笑脸，让公司形象显得有好和亲切。</p>
<h3>Atlanta Falcons</h3>
<p>这个Logo是一只隼，同时也像字母F（Falcon的首字母）。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image1.png"><img style="border-width:0px;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb1.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="229"></a></p>
<h3>Baskin Robbins</h3>
<p>这个Logo中包含了数字“31”，而“31”是他们之前的Logo。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image2.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb2.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="99"></a></p>
<h3>Chick-Fil-A</h3>
<p><span> </span>这个Logo把字母C（Chick-Fil-A的首字母）转化成了一只鸡的形象。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image3.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb3.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="126"></a></p>
<h3>City Direct</h3>
<p>这里的隐藏含义比较难发现：如果你注意Logo中的黑色部分，飞机是被字母“CD”（City Direct的缩写）包围着的。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image4.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb4.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="128"></a></p>
<h3>Eighty 20</h3>
<p>这个必须要好好解释一下：蓝色方块代表1，灰色代表0。整个logo的第一行其实是二进制数字1010000，转换成十进制就是80。而第二行是0010100，转换成十进制就是20。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image5.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb5.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="124"></a></p>
<h3>Families / Marriage</h3>
<p>在Fammilies的Logo中，字母“ili”看上去就像一家人：爸爸妈妈和孩子。</p>
<p>而在Marriage的Logo中，两个字母R对在一起，像面对面拉着手的两个人，象征了结合的夫妻二人。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image6.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb6.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="196"></a></p>
<h3>Fashion Center</h3>
<p>纽扣上的洞组成了字母F。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image7.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb7.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="88"></a></p>
<h3>FedEx</h3>
<p>有一个比较难找到的信息：字母E和x中间夹着的空白部分是个箭头，象征着联邦快递的精准与快速。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image8.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb8.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="136"></a></p>
<h3>Forkwire</h3>
<p>Forkwire是一家在线食物快递公司。Logo左部的图标是个“@”，代表在线；而这个@则由叉子和线（叉子柄的变形）组成，正好是Forkwire的缩写。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image9.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb9.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="184"></a></p>
<h3>Formula 1</h3>
<p>Logo中间夹着的白色部分是个“1”。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image10.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb10.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="113"></a></p>
<h3>Fort Worth Zoo</h3>
<p>“FW”两个字母变形成了一只大象。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image11.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb11.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="157"></a></p>
<h3>Goodwill</h3>
<p>Logo的左上部其实是个小写的字母“g”，这不仅是公司名的首字母，而且是个笑脸，让看到的人不知不决中就增加了对公司的好感。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image12.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb12.png" border="0" alt="image" width="189" height="244"></a></p>
<h3>Gotham Books</h3>
<p>书本堆成了个摩天大楼。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image13.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb13.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="89"></a></p>
<h3>Hartford Whalers</h3>
<p>Logo上部蓝色的部分是鲸鱼的尾巴，中间灰色的是字母“H”，下方绿色部分是“W”。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image14.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb14.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="184"></a></p>
<h3>LG</h3>
<p>很多人坚信LG的logo是个变形的吃豆人！</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image15.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb15.png" border="0" alt="image" width="174" height="244"></a></p>
<h3>Milwaukee Brewers</h3>
<p>这是一直棒球队的Logo。手套其实是由“M”和“b”两个字母组成</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image16.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb16.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="196"></a></p>
<h3>Missouri Westen State University</h3>
<p>Logo上的鹰是这所大学的吉祥物，另外鹰的轮廓其实就是密苏里州在地图上的形状。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image17.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb17.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="223"></a></p>
<h3>Mosleep</h3>
<p>Mosleep是一个研究睡眠问题的医生组织。Logo的上半部是字母“M”，下半部是个床。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image18.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb18.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="128"></a></p>
<h3>NBC</h3>
<p>NBC的logo是一只孔雀，而这只孔雀正代表了公司的座右铭：往前看，不要往后看（外表光鲜亮丽，里面一团糟是么⊙﹏⊙）。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image19.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb19.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="236"></a></p>
<h3>Pakuy</h3>
<p>由一个打开的盒子形成的字母“P”，同时暗示了公司是做包装行业的。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image20.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb20.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="89"></a></p>
<h3>Piano Forest</h3>
<p>远看是钢琴键，近看是树林。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image21.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb21.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="128"></a></p>
<h3>Schizophrenic</h3>
<p>schizophrenic是精神分裂的意思，logo往左看是个笑脸，往右看是个哭脸，果然精神分裂。。。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image22.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb22.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="122"></a></p>
<h3>To Beat or Not to Beat?</h3>
<p>皮带弯曲成了一个问号，正好代表着：打还是不打？</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image23.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb23.png" border="0" alt="image" width="229" height="244"></a></p>
<h3>Toblerone</h3>
<p>一只正在爬马特峰的熊。马特峰就是Toblerone巧克力的发源地。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image24.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb24.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="188"></a></p>
<h3>Unilever</h3>
<p>“U”中的每一个小图案都代表了联合利华的业务重点，如：衬衫（在右下，心的下面）表示“服装”，代表干净洗涤与穿着整洁。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image25.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb25.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="184"></a></p>
<h3>Via Rail Canada</h3>
<p>Logo的中间代表铁路。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image26.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb26.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="106"></a></p>
<h3>WSU</h3>
<p>一只美洲狮，同时包含了“WSU”三个字母。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image27.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb27.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="244"></a></p>
<h3>Yoga Australia</h3>
<p>如果你仔细看，姑娘腿和手中间的空白正好是澳大利亚大陆的形状。</p>
<p><a href="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image28.png"><img style="border:0px none;display:inline" title="image" src="http://cheweb.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/07/image_thumb28.png" border="0" alt="image" width="244" height="145"></a></p>
